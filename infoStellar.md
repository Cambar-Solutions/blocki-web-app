Stellar Hack+ at Stellar Week
Stellar Hack+ is the hackathon component of Stellar Week, a week-long builder program in Buenos Aires focused on technical development, ecosystem interoperability, and product growth. It brings together developers and founders from across LATAM to design, build, and deploy solutions using the Stellar stack.

The goal is not only to build prototypes, but to deliver measurable progress. Participants are expected to show clear technical advancement during the event, whether through new integrations, architecture improvements, or functional deployments that demonstrate the potential of Stellar in real-world contexts.

As part of the Stellar Lab, this edition of Stellar Hack+ will feature a ZK Morning, a series of sessions exploring how zero-knowledge proofs (ZKPs) can enhance privacy, scalability, and interoperability across the Stellar ecosystem. While there is no dedicated ZK track, projects that demonstrate understanding or future integration potential of ZK concepts will receive additional consideration during judging. Evaluation Framework
Each submission will be evaluated on technical merit, ecosystem relevance, and execution depth, with criteria adjusted to each track. Judges will include experienced builders, technical leads, and ecosystem contributors with expertise in product architecture, smart contracts, and Stellar integrations.
